About

12+ Years Building Systems
That Actually Work

I solve operations problems. For over a decade, I’ve designed the KPI frameworks, workflow systems, and process improvements that turn complex, high-volume operations into something measurable and repeatable. My background is in healthcare revenue cycle management, but the core of what I do — enterprise system implementation, data-driven process optimization, compliance design, and staff enablement — translates across any industry that runs on operational precision.

My deepest technical expertise is in Epic EHR — specifically Prelude, Cadence, Resolute, Benefits Engine, and Referrals. I’ve served as the module lead during system upgrades and go-live cycles, built the training infrastructure that brought entire departments up to speed, and redesigned authorization workflows that directly reduced claim denials and accelerated revenue.

The operation I oversee processes $3.6M+ annually across scheduling, verification, authorization, financial clearance, and arrivals — while sustaining 95%+ satisfaction scores. I built the first formal KPI tracking framework my department ever had, creating visibility into metrics that previously didn’t exist.

I’m looking for fully remote opportunities in operations analysis, revenue integrity, process consulting, or enterprise system implementation — roles where the value comes from knowing how complex systems actually work, not just how they look on a dashboard.
